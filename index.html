About Me (Bon Crowder)

My name is Bon Crowder and I despise Bootstrap.

What I have learned from this exercise is

  1. If you're not "into" front-end development, then something like Bootstrap might be the cheap and dirty solution you need to get a site up and running.
  2. If you need a site to look exactly like you want, then going right to the native css is your best bet.
  3. If you're building a site, you can create your own framework that will follow the design and logo guidelines of the organization and will make it easier for you to quickly create new pages that have the same look and feel.
  4. I love twitter, but I don't necessarily love everything that comes from twitter.
